Classic Vanilla Cake

Ingredients

For the Cake :

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter (room temperature)
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup whole milk

For the Vanilla Frosting

  • 1 cup unsalted butter (room temperature)
  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3-4 tbsp heavy cream

Instructions

Step 1 : Prepare the Batter

  1. Preheat your oven to 350¬∞F (175¬∞C). Grease and line two 8-inch cake pans with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, beat butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Add eggs one at a time, then mix in vanilla extract.
  4. Gradually add dry ingredients alternating with milk, mixing until combined.

Step 2 : Bake the Cake

  1. Divide the batter evenly between the two pans.
  2. Bake for 28-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  3. Let the cakes cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.

Step 3 : Make the Frosting

  1. Beat butter until creamy.
  2. Gradually add powdered sugar, then mix in vanilla and heavy cream until smooth.

Step 4 : Assemble and Decorate

  1. Place one cake layer on a plate and spread a layer of frosting.
  2. Place the second layer on top and frost the entire cake.
  3. Decorate with sprinkles or fresh fruit (optional).

Tips and Tricks üí°

  • Use room-temperature ingredients for a better texture.
  • For a richer flavor, add a pinch of almond extract.
  • Store leftovers in an airtight container for up to 3 days.
